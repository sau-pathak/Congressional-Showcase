What is RoadWatch?

RoadWatch is an AI-powered mobile application that transforms any dashcam into a road anomaly detection system. Using advanced machine learning, the app automatically identifies potholes, cracks, debris, animals, malfunctioning traffic lights, and other road hazards in real-time as you drive.

Every detection is logged, mapped, and automatically reported to local government agencies - creating a crowdsourced network of road safety monitoring that scales with the community.

The app is designed to be accessible to everyone. Users can connect modern dashcams via Bluetooth, or use our affordable $50 Raspberry Pi solution. Setup takes just minutes, and from then on, every drive contributes to safer roads.

The R.O.A.D. System

Our core technology follows the R.O.A.D. framework - a systematic approach to transforming raw dashcam footage into actionable infrastructure reports.

R

Recognize

Anomalies on the road through dashcam footage

O

Observe

Their location and severity in real-time

A

Analyze

Data holistically with machine learning

D

Deliver

Structured reports to government agencies
