The App

ClearMind for Teens

ClearMind is a cannabis education hub designed specifically for teens, parents, and schools. It cuts through misinformation by surfacing evidence-based content alongside real Colorado youth survey data — so users see not just the science, but what's actually happening in their communities.

The platform is structured around six practical pathways — teens, parents, schools, analytics, safety briefings, and crisis resources — so each audience finds relevant guidance fast, without wading through content meant for someone else.
